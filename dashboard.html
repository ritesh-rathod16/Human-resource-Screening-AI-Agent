Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform

How does your AI reduce bias in hiring?

Our system anonymizes candidate data (names, photos, demographics) and uses fairness-aware algorithms trained on diverse datasets to ensure decisions are based purely on skills and qualifications. We continuously audit our models for potential bias.

Is this compliant with hiring laws and regulations?

Yes, our system follows GDPR, EEOC, and other labor regulations. We provide documentation to help with compliance audits and maintain transparency in our decision-making processes.

Can I integrate it with my existing ATS?

Absolutely! We sync with Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, and most major applicant tracking systems through our API. Our team can help with setup and configuration.

How accurate is your resume screening?

Our AI achieves 92% accuracy in matching candidates to job requirements, verified through independent testing. You can always review and adjust the rankings as needed.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide 24/7 email support, dedicated account managers for enterprise clients, and comprehensive training resources including live webinars and documentation.
