About HiveMinds

Revolutionizing HR with AI-powered, bias-free recruitment solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2020, HiveMinds began with a simple mission: to make hiring fairer, faster, and more efficient using artificial intelligence.

After witnessing firsthand how unconscious bias affected hiring decisions and how much time HR teams spent on repetitive screening tasks, our founders set out to build a solution that would address both problems simultaneously.

Today, we serve hundreds of companies worldwide, helping them reduce hiring bias while cutting recruitment time by an average of 65%. Our team of HR experts, data scientists, and engineers continues to innovate at the intersection of technology and human resources.

Our Mission

To eliminate bias in hiring and help companies build diverse, high-performing teams.

We believe that talent is equally distributed, but opportunity is not. Our AI-powered platform levels the playing field by focusing on skills and potential rather than demographics or background.

By removing human bias from the early stages of recruitment, we enable organizations to discover talent they might otherwise overlook and give every candidate a fair chance to succeed.
